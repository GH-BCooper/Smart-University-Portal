Admissions

Andhra University offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, and doctoral programs across multiple faculties, including Arts, Science, Engineering, Management, Law, and Commerce. The admission process is designed to be transparent, merit-based, and student-friendly. Aspiring students from across India and abroad are encouraged to apply and join our vibrant academic community.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • We are committed to making the application process accessible for all students. If the admissions application fee presents a hardship for you or your family and you plan on applying for financial aid, the fee will be waived.

    Common Application

    1. Confirm that you meet at least one of the indicators of economic need and select “Yes” to the fee waiver prompt.
    2. Complete the fee waiver signature.

    Coalition Application, Powered by Scoir

    1. Confirm that you meet at least one indicator of economic need in your profile.
    2. If not eligible, enter the Andhra University fee waiver code: JH3S5Q2LX9

    Transfer Applicants

    1. See the Transfer Application Requirements for fee waiver details.

  • There is no formula for gaining admission to Andhra University. Academic accomplishment is important, but the Admissions Committee also considers leadership, character, extracurricular activities, and personal qualities.
